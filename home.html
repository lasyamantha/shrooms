Meeting the Demand Safely

In 2022, the per capita consumption of fresh mushrooms in the U.S. was approximately 3.65 pounds. This indicates a significant demand for mushrooms, and our platform is perfectly positioned to meet this demand.

Our Top Priority: Safety

With around 7,500 annual reports of poisonous mushroom ingestion in the U.S., we ensure all mushrooms on our platform are vetted for safety. Before any mushroom makes it onto our platform, foragers are required to ensure their offerings are not poisonous through our online tool. This rigorous process ensures that only safe, edible mushrooms are sold, providing peace of mind for our buyers.
